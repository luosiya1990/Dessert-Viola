About Me

Hi, I’m Elizabeth!

I’m a Chinese girl in Canada.

I love everything about food: cooking, eating and taking beautiful pictures of them. I am passionate about discovering yummy desserts and recipes.

I love the irresistible smell coming out of the oven. I love the sound of cooking. And I love the smile on people’s face when enjoying a beautiful dish.

Here, I would like to share the recipes I collect, modified or created. To share the joy of cooking and the love of food!

Ps. The name of Dessert Viola comes from the Chinese character “堇”. It is the pen name of my favorite author.
