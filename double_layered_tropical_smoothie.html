Double Layered Tropical Smoothie

Recipe from: Amanda Tastes

Original Recipe

Ingredients (for 2 servings):

Layer 1:

Papaya: 200g

Plain Yogurt: 100g

Layer 2:

Red Dragon Fruit: 100g

Banana: 100g

Plain Yogurt: 200g

1. Cut and frozen all fruits.

2. Blend all ingredients for layer 1.

3. Pour layer 1 into a glass until half full.

4. Clean blender and blend in ingredients for layer 2.

5. Gently pour layer 2 onto layer 1
